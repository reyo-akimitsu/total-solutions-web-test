Frequently asked questions

What is the typical turnaround time for orders?
Turnaround time varies based on the complexity of the order and current workload. Standard business cards take 2-3 business days, while larger orders may take 5-7 business days. Rush orders are available for an additional fee.
2
Do you offer design services?
Yes, we have a team of professional designers who can help create custom designs for your products. Whether you need a simple business card layout or a complete branding package, we can assist you.
3
What file formats do you accept for printing?
We accept PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, JPG, and PNG files. For best results, we recommend PDF files with CMYK color mode and 300 DPI resolution. Our team can also work with other formats upon request.
